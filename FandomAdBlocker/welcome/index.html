Welcome to FADBlock!
FADBlock is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience on Fandom sites by blocking intrusive ads.
To learn more about FADBlock, visit the About page.
To configure your preferences, open the Settings page.

Pin the extension to see statistics and manage your settings.
Step 1: Click the extension puzzle icon in top-right side.
Step 2: Click the pin button to pin the extension.
Step 3: That's it now you can click on the extension icon to open the popup.

If you encounter any issues, please visit the Issues page on GitHub and leave your feedback.
Thank you for choosing FADBlock!
